Privacy Preference Center
Like other apps, using Dating involves the storing and retrieval of information from your device, through trackers operated by us or our partners. We provide below a simple tool allowing you to tailor your choices as you deem fit in relation to these trackers. You can change your choices at any time in your Settings.
Manage Permissions
Personalize Marketing Permissions
These tools are used to measure and improve the effectiveness of Dating marketing campaigns on third party sites or apps.
These tools are necessary for the app to function and thus cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms.
Personalize Advertising Permissions
These tools are used to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns and make advertising more relevant to you. They perform functions like preventing the same ad from continuously reappearing, ensuring that ads are properly displayed, selecting advertisements based on your profile and measuring the number of ads displayed and their performance, such as how many people clicked on a given ad. If you do not allow these tools, the ads will be less relevant to you.
Personalize Analytics Permissions
These tools allow us to count visits and traffic sources and understand how users move around the app. This in turn helps us assess how the app performs and which features are the most and least popular so we can continue improving your experience.
