Supported Platforms and Devices
Dating is currently available on iOS, Android, and HMS devices. Along with our mobile apps, you can visit Dating.com to use Dating for Web.
We also offer a lightweight version of Dating you can take anywhere and everywhere - download Dating Lite from the Google Play store.
Dating currently supports iOS 14.0 and up, Android 7.0 and up, and the latest versions of all major web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).
